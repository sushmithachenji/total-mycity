Explore Teneti Beach Park

(Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh)

About

Teneti Park is a picturesque public park in Visakhapatnam, offering stunning views of the Bay of Bengal and the city’s coastline. Located at the foothills of Kailasagiri, the park is renowned for its peaceful ambiance, making it a popular destination for both locals and tourists, The park features well-maintained walking paths, play areas for children, and ample seating for visitors to relax and enjoy the natural beauty. Teneti Park also has several scenic viewpoints, where visitors can admire the ocean and sunset, making it a popular spot for photography enthusiasts, With its proximity to key attractions like Kailasagiri Hill and the INS Kursura Submarine Museum, the park is often visited by those exploring the city’s coastal areas. The park is named after Teneti Viswanadham, a local freedom fighter, and its development was part of Visakhapatnam’s broader effort to promote tourism and provide green spaces for recreation. The park’s combination of natural beauty and tranquil atmosphere has made it a favored location for families and individuals looking to unwind, In recent years, the local government has focused on maintaining and enhancing the park to attract more visitors, ensuring that it remains one of Visakhapatnam’s key recreational spots



History

Teneti Park was developed as part of the beautification and tourism development initiatives in Visakhapatnam. The park's history is linked with the local efforts to honor prominent personalities from the area, such as Teneti Viswanadham, after whom the park is named. Over the years, the park has evolved into a well-known tourist attraction, known for its peaceful ambiance and scenic views of the ocean and city. With continuous development and maintenance, the park has become a favorite recreational spot for both residents and visitors, contributing to the tourism appeal of Visakhapatnam.
